Gifted & Gaslit: Reclaiming the Fire

Some of us were born with minds that don’t shut up — too sharp, too alive. They called us gifted, then punished us for it. This is where we take it back.

Finding Your Voice Again

Reclaiming power starts with believing yourself again. Your perception isn’t the problem — the problem is a world terrified of clarity.

Tips for Resisting Gaslighting

  • Anchor yourself. Journal, keep records, trust your senses.
  • Stop negotiating your truth. You don’t need to convince anyone.
  • Find your misfits. One ally can drown out a room of manipulators.
  • Use your gift. Intensity, humor, art — what they tried to erase is your weapon.
  • Refuse to shrink. “Too much” is often just another word for clear.
