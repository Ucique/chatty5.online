Why Insane Stepmothers Still Haunt Us

Published on August 18, 2025

In fairy tales, stepmothers get a bad rap – they're portrayed as jealous, vain and cruel. But behind every wicked mask lies a wound. At UCIQUE we reinterpret these archetypes as symbols of transformation. Our ritual clothing and artworks confront the poison with roses and humour.

This story invites you to examine the masks you wear and to alchemise your own venom into something beautiful. Our eco-friendly pieces are dyed with plant-based colours and stitched with intentions of healing.

As you browse our collection, imagine your scars turning into embroidery, your shadows becoming art. Let’s rewrite the story: one where stepmothers – and all of us – find redemption and power.
